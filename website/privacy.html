Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Shadow Defense: Tales from the Pages collects minimal, anonymous gameplay statistics to improve the game experience. This includes:

  • Levels completed and towers used (for game balance)
  • Session duration and play patterns (for retention analysis)
  • Device type and OS version (for compatibility testing)
  • Crash reports if they occur (for bug fixing)

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists, photos, or files
  • Financial information (purchases are handled by Apple/Google)
  • Advertising identifiers

3. How We Use Information

Gameplay data is used solely to improve game balance, fix bugs, and enhance the player experience. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage

All game progress is stored locally on your device. Cloud save is an optional feature that requires explicit opt-in. If enabled, save data is encrypted in transit and stored securely.

5. In-App Purchases

Shadow Defense offers optional in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not store or have access to your payment information.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Shadow Defense does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Players who indicate they are under 13 will have all analytics features automatically disabled. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of all your data (available in Settings > Privacy > Delete All Data)
  • Opt out of data collection at any time (Settings > Privacy)
  • Request a copy of data we hold about you

8. Third-Party Services

Shadow Defense may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Game Center / Google Play Games (for achievements, optional)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play (for in-app purchases)

9. Contact

For privacy concerns or data requests, please visit defenseplanet.org or open an issue on our GitHub repository.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
