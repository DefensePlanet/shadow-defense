The Story Unfolds

The villains have escaped their stories. Only the heroes can stop them.

In Shadow Defense: Tales from the Pages, classic literary characters step off the page and into battle. Place legendary heroes as towers to defend against waves of iconic villains, each drawn from the darkest chapters of beloved stories.

From the dense forests of Sherwood to the foggy streets of Victorian London, every level brings a new world to life with procedurally generated backgrounds, themed enemies, and unique challenges. Upgrade your heroes through four powerful tiers and earn star ratings as you master each tale.

The Worlds

Six handcrafted realms, each with unique paths, enemies, and atmosphere

I

Sherwood Forest

Dense ancient woodland where outlaws reign. Navigate winding forest paths beneath towering oaks as the Sheriff's men march through the greenwood.

II

Wonderland

A topsy-turvy realm where nothing is as it seems. Twisted paths wind past oversized mushrooms and grinning cats as the Queen's forces advance.

III

Land of Oz

Follow the yellow brick road through emerald fields. The Witch's flying minions swarm the enchanted pathways in growing numbers.

IV

Neverland

A tropical paradise hiding danger at every turn. Captain Hook's pirate crew sails in from Skull Rock, raiding the island's winding shores.

V

Paris Opera

Grand halls and hidden catacombs beneath the stage. Dark figures emerge from the shadows of the labyrinthine opera house corridors.

VI

Victorian London

Fog-choked cobblestone streets under gaslight glow. Spectral figures haunt the final chapter in this atmospheric battle through Dickensian alleyways.
