Shadow Defense: Tales from the Pages

Once upon a time, the greatest characters in literature were real.

A dark force called the Shadow Author has pulled beloved heroes from their stories — Robin Hood, Alice, Dracula, Peter Pan, and more — into a world made of living ink and whispered words. Now they must fight together to escape the Tome of Shadows and save storytelling itself.

DEFEND THE PAGES
Place legendary characters as towers to defend against waves of shadow creatures. Each hero fights with abilities drawn from their original novels — Robin Hood fires arrows, the Wicked Witch summons flying monkeys, Frankenstein's Monster channels lightning, and Dracula drains the life from his enemies.

18 PLAYABLE CHARACTERS
From Sherlock Holmes to Medusa, Captain Hook to Anubis — every character is pulled from public domain literature and mythology. Each has unique attacks, upgrade paths, and a personal story arc.

3-PATH UPGRADE SYSTEM
Inspired by Bloons TD6, each character has 3 upgrade paths with 3 tiers each. Go deep in one path or spread across all three. 162 total upgrades to unlock and master.

AN EPIC STORY
4 acts of fully voice-acted narrative. Make moral choices that change your roster. Recruit villains. Free ancient gods. Discover the Narrator's true identity. Multiple endings.
