**S-Tier (game-defining, do first):**
- Portal hub world visual (#1-4)
- Villain playable characters (#51-52)
- Boss fight overhaul (#82, 107-108)
- Battle pass/seasonal content (#116)
- Wave count 20->40 (#90)

- Character voice lines (#58, 150)
