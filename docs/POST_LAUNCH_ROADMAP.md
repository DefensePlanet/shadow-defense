# Shadow Defense — Post-Launch Roadmap (#58-59)

## Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1)
**Focus:** Stability, balance, monetization baseline

- **Bug Fixes:** Address top crash reports from soft launch telemetry
- **Balance Patches:** Adjust difficulty curves based on player completion rates (target: 70% clear rate Acts 1-3, 40% Act 4)
- **Ad SDK Integration:** Rewarded video ads for bonus gold/lives (AdMob + IronSource mediation)
- **Analytics Pipeline:** Firebase events for funnel analysis (tutorial completion, first purchase, Act progression)
- **Hotfix System:** Remote config live — gold/XP multipliers, event toggles, maintenance mode

## Phase 2: Social & Competition (Month 2-3)
**Focus:** Retention through competition and community

- **PvP Arena:** Asynchronous tower defense duels — players build a defense, AI attacks opponent's layout
  - Matchmaking by account level + tower roster strength
  - Seasonal rankings with exclusive cosmetic rewards
  - 3 arena maps rotating weekly
- **Friend Leaderboards:** Weekly/all-time leaderboards for fastest clear, highest wave survived, most gold earned
  - Game Center / Google Play Games integration
  - Friend invite system via share links (shadowdefense:// URL scheme)
- **Guild System:**
  - Create/join guilds (max 30 members)
  - Guild challenges: cooperative milestones (e.g., "Guild kills 1M enemies this week")
  - Guild chat (text-only, moderated)
  - Guild leaderboard with tier rewards

## Phase 3: Content Expansion (Month 4-6)
**Focus:** New content to drive re-engagement

- **Co-op Mode:** Real-time 2-player co-op on specially designed maps
  - Shared gold pool, separate tower rosters
  - Boss fights requiring coordinated tower placement
  - Matchmaking + friend invite
- **New Characters:**
  - **Loki** (post-launch unlock via special event quest chain)
  - **Captain Ahab** (ocean-themed Act 5 preview character)
  - 2 additional characters TBD based on community polls
- **Act 5 Preview:** "The Unwritten Realms" — 9 levels teasing the next major content update
  - New enemy types: ink constructs, mirror copies of player towers
  - New mechanic: tower corruption (enemies can temporarily disable towers)

## Phase 4: Long-term Live Ops (Month 7-12)
**Focus:** Seasonal cadence, community, mod ecosystem

- **Seasonal Events:**
  - Halloween: "The Horseman's Revenge" (special maps, exclusive Horseman skin)
  - Winter: "Scrooge's Redemption" (daily advent calendar, winter-themed enemies)
  - Spring: "Alice's Garden Party" (flower-themed tower buffs, limited skins)
  - Summer: "Tarzan's Wild Hunt" (jungle survival mode, endurance challenge)
- **Mod Support:**
  - Custom level editor with Steam Workshop / sideload support
  - Moddable enemy waves, tower stats, map layouts
  - Community spotlight: featured mods in-game weekly
- **Community Features:**
  - Replay system: watch and share best runs
  - Community challenge of the week (developer-curated modifiers)
  - Fan art gallery (curated submissions displayed in-game)
  - Content creator tools: spectator mode, clean UI toggle for recording
