# Shadow Defense: Tales from the Pages — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 12, 2026

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Game progress (levels completed, characters unlocked, upgrades purchased)
- In-app purchase history (processed by Apple/Google, we do not store payment details)
- Settings preferences (volume, difficulty, accessibility options)

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system version
- Game performance data (frame rates, crash reports)
- Gameplay analytics (levels played, towers used, session duration)
- Advertising identifiers (only with your consent)

## How We Use Your Information
- To save and restore your game progress
- To improve game performance and fix bugs
- To personalize your gameplay experience
- To process in-app purchases
- To display relevant advertisements (with consent)

## Data Storage and Security
- Game saves are stored locally on your device
- Cloud saves (when enabled) use encrypted transmission
- We do not sell your personal information to third parties
- Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated

## Children's Privacy (COPPA)
Shadow Defense is rated for ages 9+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of analytics and advertising
- Export your game save data

## Advertising
We use third-party advertising partners who may collect device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

## Contact
For privacy inquiries: privacy@shadowdefensegame.com

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance.
